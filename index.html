IOSC-BVP, the inaugural Intel collaborated initiative by the Department of Electronics and Communication Engineering at Bharati Vidyapeeth's College of Engineering, embarked on its journey on November 7, 2022. The primary objective of this club is to augment students' skills in both technical and non-technical domains. IOSC-BVP was established with a forward-looking vision for growth in artificial intelligence (AI), machine learning (ML), and data-centric applications across various domains of the Internet of Things (IoT). Recognizing the industry's demand for a programming model that facilitates rapid innovation, IOSC is committed to bridging the gap between industry requirements and students' educational experiences.
